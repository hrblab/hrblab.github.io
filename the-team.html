Lab Manager

Maeve McGowan

Maeve McGowan is the current lab manager for the HRB Lab. She graduated from Lehigh University in 2025 with a Bachelor of Science in Psychology. She was an RA in the lab for a couple of years before completing her honors thesis under the mentorship of Dr. Napper. Her research focuses on health identity and parent-child communication in a variety of health contexts including alcohol use, smoking, sexual consent, and opioid use. She hopes to go to graduate school to further study health behavior change! Outside of the lab, Maeve enjoys hanging out with friends and family, listening to music, and playing pickleball.
